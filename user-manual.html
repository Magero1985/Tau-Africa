9. Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is the difference between iKbp and iKb?

A: iKbp are points for activities (10 iKbp = 1 KSH). iKb is validated value currency (1 iKb = 100,000 KSH).

Q: How do I get my referral code?

A: Your referral code is sent via email after registration and visible in your member dashboard.

Q: Can I change my membership level?

A: Yes, levels upgrade automatically based on activity and contributions.

Q: How long does value validation take?

A: Typically 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification when approved.

Q: Can I withdraw my iKbp balance?

A: Yes, subject to daily withdrawal limits based on your level.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information.
