KABIRU MINING ECOSYSTEM

Complete User Kit & Production Training Manual v1.0

1. Vision & Ecosystem Philosophy

"To build economic independence and sovereignty structures for families, communities, and generations through the simple factor of digital Value Declarations."

KABIRU is the economic engine of the TAU Africa Ecosystem. It converts dormant personal/community values into active digital currency (iKb).

2. Step-by-Step Onboarding Flow

Registration Join the TAU Africa Main App to generate your Member ID. This is your passport for all peripheral utility apps.

Input Virtually input values (assets, businesses, or family legacies) into the project via the Main Portal.

Validation Values undergo a Vigorous 3-Stage Validation (VAL1, VAL2, and VAL3) before arriving in the Mining App's Value Bank.

3. Mining Levels & Processor Rates

Mining rights are granted based on the total KSH equivalent of validated values in your Value Bank:

Mining Level Value Range (KSH) Mining Rate (Daily)
Level 1 (Basic) 1 Million < Value < 6 Million 0.005 iKb
Level 2 (Community) 5 Million < Value < 12 Million 0.01 iKb
Level 3 (Sovereign) 12 Million < Value < 24 Million 0.1 iKb

Note: Users must manually restart the mining processor every 24 hours to continue production.

4. Wallets, Conversions & Withdrawals

The Liquid Pool manages cash-to-digital currency conversion.

  • Standard Rate: 1 iKb = 100,000 Local Currency Units (KSH, UGX, BFR, TSH).
  • Withdrawals: Users can activate cash withdrawals every 30 days, coinciding with the 30-day declaration cycle.
  • Minimum Transfer: 10,000 iKbp or 0.0001 iKb.

5. Peripheral Utility Integration

Use utility apps to accumulate resources into your TAU Main Account:

  • TAU Tube: Earn iKbp (points) via content consumption and media creation.
  • TAU Marketplace: Advertise and trade goods globally using KABIRU value structures.
  • TAU Education/Health/Transport: Life-system apps for essential service generation and payment.
