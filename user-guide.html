2. Declaration Mining Concept

Understanding the Four-Step Process

Declaration Mining is built on a transparent, community-driven validation system that ensures every miner's capacity is backed by real, verifiable assets. The process consists of four essential stages that transform your real-world wealth into digital mining power.

1
Declare Your Values

In the TAU Africa Main App (accessible at https://magero1985.github.io/Tau-Africa/), you begin by declaring your real-world assets. This comprehensive declaration process captures the full spectrum of your economic value across multiple categories:

  • Physical Assets: Land parcels with title deeds, residential properties (houses, apartments), commercial buildings, vehicles (cars, motorcycles, trucks), agricultural equipment, machinery, and tools. Each physical asset should be documented with relevant proof of ownership.
  • Business Assets: Operating businesses with business registration certificates, inventory and stock, equipment and furniture, intellectual property (patents, trademarks, copyrights), digital assets, and business goodwill. Business declarations should include operational details and revenue information.
  • Skills and Services: Professional skills backed by certifications (medical degrees, engineering licenses, teaching credentials), trade skills (carpentry, plumbing, electrical work), consulting expertise, teaching and training capacity, and specialized knowledge in specific domains. Service capacity declarations should detail your hourly rates and expertise level.
  • Knowledge Assets: Educational qualifications, professional experience and track record, specialized expertise in niche areas, research and publications, mentorship capacity, and advisory services. Knowledge declarations help establish your value as a thought leader or consultant.
2
Three-Stage Validation

Your declarations undergo rigorous verification through a multi-layered validation process designed to ensure authenticity, accuracy, and community trust. This three-stage system protects the integrity of the entire KABIRU ecosystem:

  1. Internal System Review (Automated): The TAU system performs initial checks for completeness, consistency, and obvious red flags. This includes verifying that all required fields are filled, documentation is uploaded, declared values fall within reasonable ranges for the asset type, and there are no duplicate declarations in the system.
  2. Ecosystem Community Review: Fellow TAU members who serve as community validators examine your declarations. These validators check for authenticity by reviewing supporting documents, assess whether declared values are realistic based on market rates, verify that assets genuinely exist (through photos, documents, or site visits), and provide feedback or request additional information if needed.
  3. Administrative Final Approval: TAU administrators perform the final validation, conducting comprehensive review of all documentation, cross-referencing with external databases where possible, ensuring compliance with TAU policies and local regulations, and making the final determination on value assignment. Once administrators approve, your declared value is locked into your mining capacity.

Validation Timeline: The complete validation process typically takes 3 to 7 business days from initial declaration to final approval. Simple declarations with clear documentation may be approved faster, while complex declarations involving multiple assets or requiring additional verification may take the full seven days. You will receive email notifications at each stage of the validation process.

3
Value Bank Activation

Once your declarations are validated and approved, your total value (calculated in KSH equivalent) is recorded in your Value Bank within the TAU Africa Main App. This Value Bank serves as the foundation for your mining capacity in KABIRU. The system automatically assigns you to one of three mining levels based on your total validated value:

  • Level 1 - Basic Miner: For total validated values between 1,000,000 and 5,999,999 KSH. This level allows you to mine 0.005 iKb per day, which equals 500 KSH per day or approximately 15,000 KSH per month with consistent mining.
  • Level 2 - Community Miner: For total validated values between 6,000,000 and 11,999,999 KSH. This level doubles your mining capacity to 0.01 iKb per day, which equals 1,000 KSH per day or approximately 30,000 KSH per month with consistent mining.
  • Level 3 - Sovereign Miner: For total validated values of 12,000,000 KSH and above. This premium level provides the highest mining rate of 0.1 iKb per day, which equals 10,000 KSH per day or approximately 300,000 KSH per month with consistent mining.

Your mining level is automatically updated whenever you add and validate additional assets. There is no limit to how high your mining level can grow as you continue to declare and validate more value within the ecosystem.

4
Begin Mining Operations

With your Value Bank activated and mining level assigned, you are ready to begin mining KABIRU currency. Login to the KABIRU Mining App at https://magero1985.github.io/Tau-Africa/Kabiru-Mining-Portal/ and click the prominent MINE button to start your first 24-hour mining cycle. Your iKb accumulates automatically over the next 24 hours based on your mining level, with earnings distributed evenly across the cycle (hourly accumulation). You must return within 24 hours to click the MINE button again to start your next cycle‚Äîthis active participation requirement ensures miners remain engaged with the ecosystem.

    Step-by-Step Linking Process

    The Member ID linking process is designed to be straightforward and user-friendly, requiring only a few minutes to complete. Follow these detailed instructions for successful linking:

    1
    Access Profile Settings or Linking Interface

    After completing your initial login to KABIRU Mining (using your TAU Africa email and password), the system will automatically detect that your Member ID has not yet been linked. You will be presented with a linking interface‚Äîthis may appear as a popup modal, a dedicated linking page, or within your profile settings section. The interface will be clearly labeled "Link TAU Member ID" or "Connect Your TAU Account" with instructions guiding you through the process.

    If you have already logged in but did not complete the linking process, you can access it manually by clicking on your profile icon or avatar in the top right corner of the screen, selecting "Settings" or "Profile Settings" from the dropdown menu, and navigating to the "Account Linking" or "TAU Member ID" section.

    2
    Enter Your Complete Member ID

    Locate your TAU Member ID from your TAU Africa Main App account or from the welcome email you received when you registered. The Member ID follows a specific format that must be entered exactly as shown, including all hyphens and maintaining the correct letter case:

    • TAU-Skill Format: TSK-XXXXXX-XXX where XXXXXX is your unique six-digit member number and XXX is your three-digit verification code
    • TAU-Land Format: TLK-XXXXXX-XXX with the same structure as above but with TLK prefix indicating TAU-Land membership

    Type your complete Member ID carefully into the provided input field. The system may provide real-time format validation, indicating with a green checkmark when the format is correct or a red X when it detects formatting errors. Double-check that you have included the correct prefix (TSK or TLK), all hyphens in the right positions, and all digits entered correctly.

    3
    Verification and Confirmation

    Once you have entered your Member ID, click the "Link Account" or "Verify Member ID" button. The KABIRU system will immediately begin the verification process, which typically completes within five to fifteen seconds. During this time, the system performs several security and validation checks:

    • Member ID Existence Check: Verifies that the Member ID you entered actually exists in the TAU Africa database and is associated with an active account
    • Email Match Verification: Confirms that the email address you used to log into KABIRU Mining matches the email address registered with this Member ID in TAU Africa, preventing unauthorized account linking
    • Validation Status Check: Ensures that your TAU Africa account has completed asset declarations with approved validation status (minimum 1,000,000 KSH in validated value)
    • Duplicate Prevention: Checks that this Member ID has not already been linked to a different KABIRU Mining account, maintaining one-to-one account relationships
    • Data Synchronization: If all checks pass, the system pulls your complete validated value information from TAU Africa and calculates your appropriate mining level

    You will see a progress indicator or loading animation during this verification process. Do not close your browser or navigate away from the page until the process completes.

    4
    Review Confirmation Details

    Upon successful linking, the system will display a comprehensive confirmation screen showing all the details that have been established for your mining account. Review this information carefully to ensure accuracy:

    • Linked Member ID: Displays your complete Member ID that is now permanently associated with this KABIRU Mining account
    • Total Validated Value: Shows the exact total of your validated assets pulled from TAU Africa, displayed in KSH with proper formatting (e.g., "4,500,000 KSH" or "15,750,000 KSH")
    • Assigned Mining Level: Clearly indicates whether you are Level 1 (Basic), Level 2 (Community), or Level 3 (Sovereign) based on your validated value
    • Daily Mining Rate: Specifies exactly how much iKb you will produce each day (0.005 iKb, 0.01 iKb, or 0.1 iKb depending on your level)
    • Hourly Accumulation: Shows your hourly earning rate, calculated by dividing your daily rate by 24 hours, helping you understand how your earnings accumulate throughout each mining cycle

    If any information appears incorrect‚Äîfor example, if your validated value is lower than expected‚Äîreturn to TAU Africa Main App to verify that all your asset declarations have been properly validated. Contact TAU support if you believe there is an error in the system's calculation or data retrieval.
